# RecompDaily

Your daily recomp coach — an AI-assisted fitness log for men who lift.
RecompDaily helps you track food, training, and body weight day-to-day,
and uses a data-driven AI coach to suggest adjustments toward your recomp
goals (simultaneous fat loss and muscle retention / gain).

The app is currently in private beta on iOS and Android. General availability
is planned for 2026.

## Core features

- **Daily macros dashboard** — protein-first, honest calorie / macro targets.
- **Fast logging** — food photo → AI-estimated macros; text or voice input.
- **Weight trend** — simple daily weigh-in and smoothed trend line.
- **AI coach** — data-driven, direct, no pep-talk. Remembers your profile
  and recent logs when suggesting adjustments.
