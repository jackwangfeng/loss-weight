# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-04-23**

RecompDaily ("the app", "we") is an AI-assisted fitness logging tool.
This page explains what data we collect, why, how we use it, and what
rights you have.

## 1. Data we collect

### From Google Sign-In (when you choose it)

- Your **email address**
- Your **name** (as returned by Google)
- Your Google account's stable **subject identifier** (`sub`)

We do **not** receive or store your Google password.
We do **not** request access to your Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Contacts,
or any other Google data beyond your basic profile.

### Data you enter in the app

- **Body measurements**: height, weight, target weight, activity level, age.
- **Food logs**: food names, portions, estimated calories and macronutrients;
  food photos you choose to upload for AI recognition.
- **Training logs**: exercise type, duration, notes.
- **Chat messages** with the AI coach.

### Technical metadata

- Request IP address (recorded in server access logs for abuse prevention).
- Device locale (to localize AI replies).
- App version, build number.

We do **not** collect location, contacts, SMS, calendar, camera roll beyond
what you explicitly select, or third-party identifiers for advertising.

## 2. How we use it

- To compute your daily calorie and macro targets.
- To let the AI coach give advice informed by your recent logs and profile.
- To persist your account so you can access your history across devices.
- To send food photos you submit to Google's Gemini API for recognition.
- To diagnose bugs and prevent abuse.

## 3. Third parties

- **Google (Gemini API)** — receives food photos and chat prompts for AI
  processing. Google's terms apply to their service; we do not share your
  account identity with them.
- **Google (Sign-In)** — handles authentication when you choose Google login.
- **Amazon Web Services** — hosts our backend and database (region:
  Singapore, ap-southeast-1).

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** share it with advertisers.

## 4. Retention

- Active account data is retained as long as your account exists.
- Server access logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging and
  abuse prevention.
- If you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days
  from active systems and within 90 days from backups.

## 5. Your rights

You can:

- **Export your data** — email us and we will provide a JSON dump.
- **Delete your account** — email us at `14078271@qq.com` with the subject
  `Delete my RecompDaily account` from the address associated with your
  account. Deletion is processed within 30 days.
- **Request a correction** — email us; we will update incorrect fields.

## 6. Children

RecompDaily is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect
data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data
to us, email us and we will remove it.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted at the top
("Last updated" date) and, where required, announced in the app.
